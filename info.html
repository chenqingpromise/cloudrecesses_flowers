Cloud Recesses

云深不知处 · Gusu · Wei Ying, come back to Gusu with me.

The Cloud Recesses (云深不知处, Yúnshēn Bùzhīchù) is the residence of the Gusu Lan Clan located on a remote mountain outside the city of Gusu. It is connected by rivers flowing throughout the town. The Chinese name actually translates to "Somewhere unknown deep in the clouds."
